About HackForge



The "Hackforge Hackathon" is a dynamic initiative by the "Coders Club", in collaboration with WebWalk Infosys, Madurai, and officially registered under the "International Association of Engineers (IAENG)". The primary goal of this event is to inspire students from the "Department of Computer Science and Information Technology" to participate in prestigious national-level hackathons, such as the "Smart India Hackathon (SIH)".
This 24-hour coding competition is themed "AI 2.0: The Future of Intelligent Automation and Emerging Tech Trends", providing a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. Participants will work on problem statements sourced from SIH 2023 and 2024, ensuring they engage with real-world challenges that require practical and innovative solutions. This hackathon allows students to refine their technical expertise, gain hands-on experience in working under pressure, collaborate effectively with peers, and implement cutting-edge technologies to build functional prototypes.
By participating in the "Hackforge Hackathon", students will not only enhance their teamwork, leadership, and critical thinking skills but also develop a deep understanding of industry-relevant problem-solving techniques. The event fosters an environment of healthy competition and innovation, encouraging participants to explore emerging technologies to solve complex challenges.


Exciting Benefits for Participants:


Internship Opportunities: Best performers will have the chance to secure internships at WebWalk Infosys.
Academic Credits: Participants will earn 2 experiential elective credits upon completion of the hackathon.
Industry Exposure: The hackathon serves as a stepping stone for students aspiring to compete at national and international levels, equipping them with the skills to tackle multifaceted problems with confidence and efficiency.
